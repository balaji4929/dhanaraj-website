The complete collection

The Archive

Forty-four years of Rainbow Puppetry, gathered in one place. Alongside the photographs sit the original printed invitations and programme sheets — from village churches, schools, universities and government campaigns — each one recording a puppet show by Dr. Dhanaraj. The collection continues to grow.

The archive is being prepared. Photographs from forty-four years of Rainbow Puppetry are currently being gathered, sorted and captioned. They will appear here as the collection is completed.
